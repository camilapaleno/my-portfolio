Recruitment


Your Bruin Polyglot Society experience will be simply unforgettable.


Are you someone who perseveres under challenging situations? Are you a student who is determined to not just learn, but acquire a non-native language? If so, then apply to our organization! We are actively looking for UCLA’s brightest students who are committed to reaching their full potential.


To ensure a diverse representation of undergraduates in our organization, Bruin Polyglot Society’s recruitment process is highly selective. Our members, whose majors include the likes of Global Studies, Mathematics, and Psychology, hail from a vast number of different countries.


Please familiarize yourself with our recruitment schedule for Winter Quarter 2021. We appreciate your interest in Bruin Polyglot Society!

General Member


No application is required for this position. You will be subscribed to our listserv and will receive newsletters with language resources and tips.



Open Form

Associate


The application and interview is required for this position. If selected, you will have access to all of our language resources and receive personal guidance from board members. We are looking for motivated students who are willing to put in the work to reach proficiency in their target language. Prior exposure to the target language is not required.

Language Tutor


The application and interview is required for this position. We encourage anyone who is proficient or near-native in a world language to apply for this role. You will receive all the associate benefits and will teach lessons to any interested UCLA students.

Growth Lead


The application and interview is required for this position. If selected, you will play an integral role in the marketing aspects of BPS. You will collaborate with others to increase our reach and engagement and spread the BPS message across campus and beyond. You will receive all the associate benefits.
