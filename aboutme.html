ABOUT ME

Hello! My name is Camila (cam-ee-la).

I was born and raised in West Los Angeles and currently attend UCLA as a psychology student.

I have been taking portrait & landscape photos since I was in middle school. My love for landscape photography inspired me to travel to Iceland when I was 17. Since then, I have worked hard to travel to new countries. My next trip is to Vietnam in 2021!

I've also worked for a marketing agency creating content for social media and websites. This inspired me to learn web development. I am currently taking a quarter off from school to learn more coding languages for web development. So far I can confidently use html and css.
